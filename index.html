About Us

BIMARCH Studio is an international architectural and BIM services firm, delivering cutting-edge solutions in design, planning, and project visualization. Our expertise spans across commercial, residential, industrial, and hospitality projects.

With a keen focus on innovation and efficiency, we empower our clients by leveraging the latest in Building Information Modeling (BIM) technologies. Our team of highly skilled architects and BIM professionals ensures that every project is executed with precision and creativity.

We collaborate with clients in the USA, UK, Dubai, Australia, and Singapore, providing tailored architectural and BIM solutions that drive excellence in design and execution.
